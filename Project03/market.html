[A] Gwangjang Market

Gwangjang Market is one of the oldest and largest traditional markets in Korea with more than 5,000 shops. It is famous for stree food.
Hours: 9AM - 6PM
Transportation: Jongno-(5)ga Station (Line1) exit 8
Famous food: Sundae, Mayak gimbap, Tteokbokki, Bindaetteok, raw beef

[B] Dongdaemun Market

Dongdaemun is a large commercial district comprising traditional markets.
Hours: 10AM - 5AM (next day)
*Opearting hours will vary by shop
Transportation: Jongkak Station (Line1) exit 12
Shopping Malls: Migliore valley, Doota, Gwanghee Fashion mall, Pyounghwa clothing market, Hello aPM

[C] Tongin Traditional Market

Tongin Market is a traditional market that is located west of Gyeongbokgung palace and was eastablished in 1941. It is famous for buying street food with special coin called 'Yeonjeon'.
Hours: 11AM - 4PM
Transportation: Gyeongbokgung Station (Line3) exit 2
