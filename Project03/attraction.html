[A] Gyeongbokgung palace

Gyeongbokgung is the largetst palace in Seoul. It was built in 1395 in Joseon dynasty.
Hours: 9AM - 6PM (Mar-May)
Transportation: Gyeongbokgung Station (Line3) exit 5
Events: Royal Gate Ceremony (11:00/13:00)
Fees: 5,000(won) - 10,000(won)
Website: official webiste

[C] Bukchon Hanok Village

Bukchon Hanok village is a Korean traditional village in Seoul. It is located between Gyeongbokgung palace and Changdeokgung Palace.
Transportation: Gyeongbokgung Station (Line3) exit 5
Phone: +82) 02-2148-4160
Fees: Free
Website: official webiste
